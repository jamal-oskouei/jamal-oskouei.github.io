About

Jamal Oskouei (born 21 March 1967 in Isfahan, Iran) is an Iranian movie and animation director and editor. He lives in Tehran and has experienced directing documentary movies and animations that have nominated and won awards in different festivals around the world.

Early life and work

Oskouei, started learning 8-millimeter filmmaking guided with Mohammadreza Abedi in Institute for the Intellectual Development of Children and Young Adults at 14 and started making animations at the age 15. His first animation The Old Pain that was filmed on 8-millimeter film won the best animation award from the "cinema of the young festival" of Tehran in 1986.

His next animation Duel on table won the "second student festival"s best animation award in 1988. His first 16-millimeter animation was made in Documentary and Experimental Film Center. He graduated from Iran Broadcasting University in 1994. Started his career in IRIB of Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province.
